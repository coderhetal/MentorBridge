About MentorBridge

Started by VNIT alumni. Built for every junior.

We graduated from NIT Nagpur in 2025 and looked back at our own JEE journey. The thing that made the real difference wasn't the resources — it was that one senior who said, "bhai ye mat karo, ye karo."

Most 11th and 12th students preparing for JEE never get that conversation. They have material, but no clarity. Effort, but no direction. We built MentorBridge to change that.

We're starting small, staying personal, and making sure every student gets a mentor who's actually been through their exact situation. Our mentors volunteer their time — there's no commercial interest behind this platform.

The best guidance comes from someone who remembers what it felt like to not know the answer.
🌐

MentorBridge is an independent, non-commercial mentorship initiative. All mentors contribute voluntarily in their personal capacity. This platform is not affiliated with any organisation, institution, or employer.
